BAHAGIAN 1: SENARIO (VIGNETTE)

Sila baca petikan di bawah dengan teliti sebelum menjawab soalan di Bahagian seterusnya.
Please read the following passage carefully before answering the questions in the next Section.

"Rosli adalah seorang lelaki berusia 30 tahun. Sejak kebelakangan ini, Rosli mula berkelakuan pelik. Dia sering mendengar suara-suara yang orang lain tidak dengar dan bercakap sendirian. Dia juga mendakwa bahawa dia mempunyai kuasa luar biasa dan sering merasa curiga bahawa orang di sekelilingnya mahu mencederakannya. Rosli tidak lagi menguruskan kebersihan dirinya dan jarang mandi. Dia telah didiagnosis menghidap Schizophrenia dan telah beberapa kali dimasukkan ke wad psikiatri."

"Rosli is a 30-year-old man. Recently, Rosli has started to behave strangely. He often hears voices that others do not hear and talks to himself. He also claims to have extraordinary powers and is often suspicious that people around him want to harm him. Rosli no longer takes care of his personal hygiene and rarely bathes. He has been diagnosed with Schizophrenia and has been admitted to a psychiatric ward several times."
